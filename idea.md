# Facebook Ads Creative Intelligence

## Idea
Launch an actor that scrapes Facebook Ads Library and delivers creative-level intelligence: ad angles, format mix, CTA patterns, and competitor ad fatigue signals.

## Why this is trending/sellable
- `facebook-ads-library-scraper` has `1805` users in 30 days and paid per-item pricing.
- Useful for agencies, e-commerce brands, and media buyers who need continuous monitoring.

## How to implement
1. Inputs: advertiser pages, keywords, geo, ad status, date range.
2. Extraction: ad text, media type, CTA, landing page, active dates.
3. Processing: classify ad angle, hook style, and offer type.
4. Trend layer: track new creatives and ad volume changes over time.
5. Output: dataset + weekly summary object for BI tools.
6. Scheduling: preconfigured daily and weekly run presets.

## Monetization
- `PRICE_PER_DATASET_ITEM` for high-volume users.
- Premium monthly package with saved watchlists and alerting.
